FAQ

Honest answers to the questions we hear most.

Can't see your question? Phone the office on 033 330 5257

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes — Midlands Hospice is a registered NPO (002-413). Please email us for a Section 18A receipt.

How does PayFast keep my card safe?

PayFast is a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor used by thousands of South African organisations. Your card details never touch our servers — they go directly to PayFast's encrypted gateway.

Can I donate by EFT or in person?

Absolutely. Please contact us via email - admin@midlandshospice.org.za, or you're welcome at the office Mon–Fri, 8:00am–4:00pm.

What happens once the building is finished?

The improved depot lets us process about three times the donated stock, which translates directly into more home visits.
